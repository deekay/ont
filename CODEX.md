# Codex Working Directive

This repository uses a daily working log so ONT decisions and progress do not
disappear into chat context.

## Daily Status Discipline

For every meaningful ONT work session:

1. Keep the current day's log at `docs/status/daily/YYYY-MM-DD.md`.
2. Add entries when a decision is made, a material implementation/review step
   lands, a blocker appears, or an open question becomes sharper.
3. Prefer concise bullets over transcript-style notes.
4. Record uncertainty honestly. Use `Decision`, `Progress`, `Open question`,
   `Risk`, `Verification`, and `Follow-up` labels when useful.
5. Do not wait until end of day to capture important context.

When asked for a daily summary, synthesize the current day's log into:

- executive summary
- decisions made
- progress completed
- open questions needing group feedback
- proposed next-day focus

The daily summary should be written for humans following ONT progress in the
group chat: clear, short, and specific enough that they can weigh in.

## Current Project Posture

The current lead launch direction is universal public bonded auctions for every
valid ONT name. Avoid reintroducing reserved-name lists, direct-allocation lanes,
pre-launch reservations, or short-name waves unless the design is explicitly
reopened.

Treat exact auction windows, opening floors, increment strengths, and maturity
duration as reviewable launch parameters until they are intentionally frozen.
